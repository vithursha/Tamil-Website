Lesson 1

First we are going to learn how to recognise Vowels in Tamil.They are called "Uyir Ezhuthugal" (உயிர் எழுத்துகள்).

The double letters in the pronounciation denote that they are twice as long as the previous letter.

Tamil Pronounciation Example Sound
அ a ant, angle x
ஆ aa ham, arm x
இ i tin, ring x
ஈ ii seed, eagle x
உ u put, pull x
ஊ uu root, soon x
எ e end, enemy x
ஏ ae ape, ace x
ஐ ai ice, nice x
ஒ o rod, onion x
ஓ oa dog,coal x
ஔ ow now, shovel x
ஃ q stomach x
