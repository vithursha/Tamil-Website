Lesson 2

Having learned the "Uyir Ezhuthugal" we are now going to look at the "Mei Ezhuthugal" (மெய் எழுத்துகள்). They can be considered as the consonants when you compare it to English.

You will probably notice that letters such as "ண்", "ந்" and "ன்" all denote the letter "n" and letters "ர்" and "ற்" both denote "r" and "ல்", "ழ்" and "ள்" all denote to letter "l". For now, it will be easy for you to believe that they all sound similar. We will go in depth in the following lessons when we look at the individual letters and their examples.
